# Best Resources and Platforms

The key to passing a SQL interview is raw volume. Reading theory is essential, but building muscle memory by writing actual queries in diverse schemas is the only way to solidify your knowledge. 

Here are the best, curated platforms and resources to practice for your technical screening:

---

## Interactive Practice Platforms

1. **[LeetCode (Database Section)](https://leetcode.com/problemset/database/)**
   - *Best for:* Standardized difficulty, massive community discussion, and direct translation to what FAANG companies hand out on whiteboards.
   - *Focus on:* The "Top SQL 50" study plan.
2. **[DataLemur](https://datalemur.com/)**
   - *Best for:* Hyper-realistic, real-world questions taken straight from the interviews of tech giants (Facebook, Google, Uber, Amazon).
   - *Highlights:* The scenarios feel like actual product questions.
3. **[StrataScratch](https://www.stratascratch.com/)**
   - *Best for:* Data Science and Data Analyst specific roles. They provide datasets similar to what you'd see in Pandas/SQL hybrid interviews.
4. **[HackerRank (SQL Section)](https://www.hackerrank.com/domains/sql)**
   - *Best for:* Grinding fundamentals. If you feel rusty on basic syntax or tricky standard joins, HackerRank's ramp-up curve is excellent.

## Recommended Reading

1. **[Use The Index, Luke!](https://use-the-index-luke.com/)**
   - The absolute gold standard guide to database performance, indexing, and writing *fast* queries. Essential reading for Backend and Data engineers.
2. **[Mode SQL Tutorial](https://mode.com/sql-tutorial/)**
   - A brilliant, interactive, and beautifully designed course that bridges the gap from beginner to advanced analytical SQL.

## High-Quality YouTube Channels

1. **[Luke Barousse](https://www.youtube.com/c/LukeBarousse)** 
   - Great holistic reviews of the Data Analyst interview process and SQL live-coding.
2. **[Alex The Analyst](https://www.youtube.com/c/AlexTheAnalyst)**
   - Offers robust bootcamp boot-camp style walk-throughs covering everything from basic installations to portfolio-level SQL design projects.
3. **[TechTFQ](https://www.youtube.com/c/techTFQ)**
   - Incredibly specialized channel entirely focused on advanced SQL interview questions, particularly Window Functions and tricky CTEs.
